Trump Game- Did he say that?

Welcome to something useless, but hopefully fun

Thank you for stopping by this page. This is a quick game for those who want to test their Trump quote knowledge. Below is a quick explanation of how the game works.

  • Once you click play you will see a quote, and have to click True or False to whether or not this was actually said by Donald Trump.
  • If you get it right it will counted in your correct answers near the bottom, and if you get it wrong it will be put as a strike against you.
  • Once you get three strikes you will lose the game.

Good luck, and may the odds be ever in your favor.
